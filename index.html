—Teachzy Highlights
Teachzy is an efficient communication platform designed specifically to meet Teacher’s and Student’s need of a structured academic environment online.

A communication application designed specifically for teachers and students catering their need to stay connected outside classrooms . The application design makes it easily accessible for anyone who is a stakeholder in the education.
It offers a platform to communicate, interact and stay updated with the ongoing curriculum. Also reduces the amount of time teachers have to put in to maintain lists and make entries.
